DURGA PUJA 2022


Durga Puja, also known as Durgotsava or Sharodotsava, is an annual Hindu festival originating in
the Indian subcontinent which reveres and pays homage to the Hindu goddess Durga and is
also celebrated because of Durga's victory over Mahishasur.
